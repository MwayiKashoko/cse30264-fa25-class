Emergency Information

Current Status

There is no emergency at the University of Notre Dame. The campus is operating under normal conditions.

In the event of an emergency, this site will be updated with information about the nature of the incident, how to remain safe and, after the event, the point at which safety has been restored.

In case of any emergency call:

  • (574) 631-5555 (from a cell phone)
  • 911 (from a campus phone)

If on University-related travel and experiencing an emergency contact:
Crisis24 at +1 (443) 569-8607 or via the assistance button in the Crisis24 Horizon mobile app
